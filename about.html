Our Story

From a small fitness studio to Nagpur’s fitness movement.

Built For People Who Refuse To Stay Average

Founded in 2020, The Muscle Hustle started with a simple vision: create a gym where beginners feel motivated, athletes feel challenged, and everyone feels unstoppable. What began as a compact training space quickly evolved into one of Nagpur’s fastest-growing fitness destinations. Today, members train using premium imported equipment, expert-designed workout systems, and a supportive community built around discipline, consistency, and transformation.

Meet The Founder

The vision behind The Muscle Hustle.

Armaan Khanna

Armaan Khanna started his fitness journey as a teenager determined to transform his life through discipline and training. After years of bodybuilding experience and coaching, he realized that most gyms lacked motivation, guidance, and community. That vision led to the creation of The Muscle Hustle — a premium fitness space where anyone can build strength, confidence, and a winning mindset. Today, his mission continues to inspire thousands of people across Nagpur to chase a stronger version of themselves.
